# Your Profile

Access your profile by clicking your avatar in the top-right corner of the panel and selecting **My Profile**.

## Available Settings

### Personal Information
Update your **name** and **email address**. Changes are saved immediately.

### Password
Change your password at any time. You will need to enter your current password for confirmation.

### Browser Sessions
View all your active sessions across different browsers and devices. You can revoke any session you don't recognize to keep your account secure.

### Language
Switch between available languages:
- **English** (en)
- **Portuguese - Brazil** (pt_BR)

All labels, navigation groups, and system messages will update to your chosen language.

### Theme Color
Customize the primary accent color of the admin panel to your preference. This setting is personal and does not affect other users.
